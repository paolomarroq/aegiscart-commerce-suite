AegisCart Final Brand Direction

Selected route: Proposal 1 with colors from Proposal 2.

Logo: shopping cart + shield + motion lines.
Reason: The previous proposals did not communicate e-commerce strongly enough. This version uses a visible cart as the main visual anchor while keeping protection/monitoring through the shield.

Positioning: AegisCart is software for e-commerce operations, not an online store.
Tagline: Ecommerce Management Suite.
Core message: Manage, monitor and trace e-commerce operations from one platform.
